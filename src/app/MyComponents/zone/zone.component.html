This particular room of concentration which will serve to take you in a completely different zone is inspired by 'pyrobrain'.
This is basically a pyramid of 4 sides, each displaying a lobe of our brain which in turn holds its own specific significance.
This particular process aims to help better the concentration.
On each slide, the symbolic figure of one lobe has been boldly displayed.
At a predetermined time, concentration has to be done for 2 minutes on the bold symbol of all slides successively.
During the same, one must think of his/her goal and the plan of action.
The lobes are in the following order:
a. Parietal
b. Temporal
c. Brainstem
d. Cerebellum
e. Frontal
f. Occipital
